Blizzard's Hearthstone is a very popular digital card game in the style of "Magic: The Gathering". For each match the player selects a hero character and a deck of 30 cards. These can be choosen from a selection of over 300 cards. In battle the enemy hero has to be eliminated using the minions and spells on the cards.

The high number of cards allows for a lot of cool combinations of cards. Hearthstone Comboland provides a platform to share, vote on and discuss those combinations.
